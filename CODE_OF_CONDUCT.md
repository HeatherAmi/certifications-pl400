# Code of Conduct

This is a learning community. Be kind, be patient, and be helpful.

## Our pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive environment for learners at every level, from career switchers and complete beginners to experienced developers.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that helps:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of behaviour that will not be tolerated:

- Personal attacks or insulting comments
- Trolling, deliberately disruptive comments, or harassment
- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Enforcement

Issues can be reported by opening a private issue or contacting me via [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/heather-ami-digital/). All reports will be reviewed and acted on as appropriate.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
